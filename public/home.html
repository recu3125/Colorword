Colorword

what color comes to your mind from the word "intelligence"?
there is a tendency in the colors people associate with a word, as many people answer blue-like colors to it.
Did you also think of the same color? No? Aren't you curious about what colors other people usually think of?

Colorword is a project website where people gather colors that come to mind from words and make them into a single picture of color combinations!

Tips

  • Answer all the words to suggest new words! You will receive a link to suggest new words to be added to this website when you answer all the words.
  • Avoid selecting extreme colors at the edges of the color picker, such as 100% white or black.
  • Choose your colors carefully and try to find the exact color in your mind. Strive for perfection.
